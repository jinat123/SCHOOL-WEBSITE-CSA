Admissions

About Admissions

The Admissions Office of Colegio San Agustin Makati is the welcoming gateway for students and families seeking quality Catholic education. We are committed to guiding applicants through a smooth and transparent admissions process, beginning with online application and document submission, followed by entrance testing and evaluation. Our team ensures each applicant is fairly assessed based on academic performance, conduct, and alignment with Augustinian values. We support families at every stage, from inquiry to enrollment, promoting a nurturing and faith-driven environment where students grow intellectually, morally, and spiritually. Your journey with CSA Makati starts here.

Admissions Policy

âˆ’ As a Catholic School

As a Catholic school, CSA holds strongly on the Catholic faith and its practices which must be complied with. Thus, students and parents are expected to become actively involved or express full support espoused by the school.

+ As a School for Student Leaders

As a school for student leaders, only students with satisfactory academic qualifications, of good moral standing and with socially acceptable conduct will be considered candidates for admission. In this regard, all student applicants/returnees, for K-11, should have a general average (GPA) of 85% and conduct grade not lower than B or its equivalent must be maintained.

+ As a Basic Education and Accredited School

As a Basic Education and Accredited School, CSA is managed and administered in accordance with relevant government policies and conforms with applicable national and international standards and regulations. In adherence to DepEd order no 47, series of 2016, the school strictly implements a cut-off age for incoming Nursery, Junior Kinder Senior Kinder and Grade 1 applicants. Also, for Grade 11 STEM strand, as per DepEd order no 55, series of 2016, there will be a required cut-off score. The Final Grade both in Science and Math in Grade 10 should be 85 and above. Learners should also have at least a percentile rank of 86 and above in the STEM subtest in the career assessment exam. The studentâ€™s acceptance/registration in the school, therefore, is considered an expression of his/her willingness to abide by all its rules and regulations.

+ Admission to CSA Makati is Conditional

Admission to CSA Makati is conditional. CSA reserves the right to refuse acceptance or re-admission to any student whose behavior and academic standing are found not in conformity with or do not meet the standards of the school. Students with special needs may apply provided an official certification from a specialist with the specific needs and intervention is submitted for proper accommodation.
